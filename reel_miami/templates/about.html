The mission of Reel Miami is to promote film culture in Miami-Dade County. It is born out of a lack of a centralized resource for art house film in the region.

The website achieves this mission primarily by listing all specialty film screenings located throughout Greater Miami.

In keeping with the spirit of the non-profit art house, this website is entirely open source. The code can be reviewed here.
